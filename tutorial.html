How to transcribe the 2014 Referendum Leaflets

This is a very simple application for the crowd-sourcing of data from the yes and no groups leafelts for the 2014 Scottish Referendum. This project is on behalf of the Scottish Political Archive, housed at the University of Stirling. Within this project, all we need is the content of the leaflet to be transcribed and the presence of graphic elements to be annotated and described.

The fields we would like you to transcribe are:

  • The body of the leaflet
  • The precence of graphic elements
  • Comments

That seems simple!
